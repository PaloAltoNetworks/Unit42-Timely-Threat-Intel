2024-08-07 (WEDNESDAY): DOMAINS IMPERSONATING POSTAL SERVICES

AUTHOR:

- 

NOTES:

- Since late last year, we have been monitoring phishing campaigns impersonating various postal services across the world.
- We find many newly-registered domains (NRDs) every day from these campaigns.
- These campaigns often use SMS messages (known as "smishing") to send malicious URLs as an initial attack vector.
- We often see more than 200 hits on  malicious domains impersonating postal services each day.
- Although these domains mostly impersonate US postal services, we also find other nations like:
  - Australia
  - Brazil
  - Canada
  - India
  - Ireland
  - Israel
  - United Kingdom
